# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## Pricing & Features

### Basic Plan
* **Price**: $29/month
* **Video Limit**: 10 videos/month
* **Resolution**: 720p resolution
* **Additional Features**: Standard editing tools, export with watermark, standard email support.

### Pro Plan
* **Price**: $79/month
* **Video Limit**: Unlimited videos
* **Resolution**: 4K resolution
* **Additional Features**: AI captions, advanced transitions, priority rendering, no watermark.

## Company Policies

* **Refunds**: No refunds after 7 days of purchase.
* **Support**: 24/7 support is available only on the Pro plan. Basic plan users get standard email support during business hours.
